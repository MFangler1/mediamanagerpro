Frequently asked questions

What is MediaManagerPro?

MediaManagerPro is a comprehensive social media management platform that allows you to manage, schedule, and analyze your social media content across multiple platforms from one unified dashboard.

How does the AI-powered content creation work?

Our AI-powered content creation tool uses advanced algorithms to help you generate engaging and relevant content for your social media posts. You can also create viral campaigns, photo competitions, and testimonials with ease.

Can I schedule posts for multiple platforms at once?

Yes, you can schedule posts for multiple platforms at once using our smart scheduling and automation features. This includes setting up automatic reposting queues and RSS feed automation.

What kind of analytics does MediaManagerPro provide?

MediaManagerPro provides advanced analytics that allow you to track the performance of your posts across all platforms. You can monitor engagement, reach, and growth metrics in real-time.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a free 7-day trial that allows you to experience all the features of MediaManagerPro. No credit card is required, and you can cancel anytime.

What happens after the free trial?

After the free trial, you can choose from one of our flexible pricing plans that best fit your social media management needs and budget. You can continue using MediaManagerPro with all its features, or choose a plan that offers additional services like content creation and campaign management.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time by going to your account settings and selecting the cancel option. You will continue to have access to MediaManagerPro until the end of your billing cycle.

Is my data safe with MediaManagerPro?

Yes, your data is safe with MediaManagerPro. We use enterprise-grade security features, regular backups, and comply with industry standards to ensure that your data is protected.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. If you find that you need more features or user accounts, you can upgrade to a higher plan. Similarly, if you need to reduce your usage, you can downgrade to a lower plan.

What support is available?

We offer 24/7 support via email, and phone support during business hours. Additionally, we provide comprehensive video training and resources to help you get the most out of MediaManagerPro.
